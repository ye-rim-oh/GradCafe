### Key Takeaways

1. **Acceptance rates stayed in a compressed band**: 35.1% to 45.9% across 2020-2026.
2. **Nationality gap remains persistent**: American acceptance is higher in most years, with 2022 as the notable exception.
3. **GRE participation is uneven**: average scores are relatively stable while reporting volume moves substantially year by year.
4. **Decision timing is concentrated**: most seasons still cluster in early-to-mid February.
5. **2026 is still a moving snapshot**: additional posts and waitlist movement can still shift aggregate figures.

## Notes and Caveats

- All seasons (2020-2026) were re-scraped on March 4, 2026 with one parser workflow.
- Unknown values indicate records where users did not report nationality or subfield badges.
- All volume and acceptance rate calculations use Accepted and Rejected for the denominator; Interview and Waitlist are excluded by design.
- Because data is self-reported, results should be interpreted as directional trends rather than official statistics.
